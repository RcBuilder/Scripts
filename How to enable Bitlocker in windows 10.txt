How to enable Bitlocker in windows 10
-------------------------------------

support:
ALL editions excepts of 'Home' edition 

--

enable bitLocker:
1. Control Panel > BitLocker Drive Encryption
2. Choose the drive to encrypt > click on 'Turn on BitLocker'
3. checked 'Use a password..' option > Set Password
4. save Recovery password to file (optional)
5. 
--

check bitLocker status:
[Cmd command]
> manage-bde - status [drive]

// sample
> manage-bde - status D:
