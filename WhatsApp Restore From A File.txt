WhatsApp Restore From A File
----------------------------
1. connect your device and copy the db to restore to the databases folder 
   sdcard/WhatsApp/Databases  

2. change the name to 'msgstore.db.crypt12'
   note: you can get one of the automatic backups (e.g: msgstore-YYYY-MM-DD.1.db.crypt12)

3. uninstall and reinstall WhatsApp.

4. tap RESTORE when prompted.
