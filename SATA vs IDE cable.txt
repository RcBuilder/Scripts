SATA vs IDE cable
-----------------
IDE aka PATA
SATA aka ATA

- SATA is smaller and has 7 pins whereas IDE has 40 pins.
- SATA cable is longer (up to 1m) than the IDE (45cm).
- SATA can connect only 1 HDDs whereas IDE can connect 2 HDDs. 
- SATA transfers data range of 150mb/sec to 300mb/sec whereas IDE uses 5mb/sec to 133mb/sec.
