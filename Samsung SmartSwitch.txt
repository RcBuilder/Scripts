Samsung SmartSwitch - Copy Device Content
-----------------------------------------
Transfer your data from your old android to your new Galaxy
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nsEwoo29lX0

(steps)
1. Connect Devices - launch SmartSwitch in both source and destination devices 
   -  in the old device > tap 'Send data'
   -  in the new device > tap 'Receive data' > choose 'Galaxy' or 'Android'
2. Choose transfer type - Wifi or Cable
3. in the old device > Select Data to transfer > Transfer
