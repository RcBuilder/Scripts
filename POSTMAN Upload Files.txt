POSTMAN Upload Files
--------------------
1. launch postman 
2. set verb as POST
3. Headers (tab) > set Content-Type as 'multipart/form-data' or 'x-www-form-urlencoded'
4. Body (tab) > form-data > under 'KEY' column choose a 'File' (instead of Text) 
   note! a DDL appears at the right end of the input
5. under 'VALUE' column > click select and upload a file
