Google Business Reviews Widget
------------------------------
usage:
see 'https://rcb.co.il' 

-

(steps)
1. create a new 'elfsight' account:
   https://apps.elfsight.com/panel/applications/
   note! or choose other provider 
  
2. applications > add >  choose 'Google Reviews' widget
3. select a template (e.g: Floating Badge)
4. find your business source by providing your google place-id 
   note! to find your business id, see 'find your business id'
5. configure your widget (source, layout, review, appearance, settings)
6. apply and save (top-right corner)
7. once configured - click on 'Add to website' button (Installation tab)
8. copy code and paste it within your website

-

note that once planted within your site html, 
all widget settings are controlled from 'elfsight' dashboard!
any change will update the widget on your website.
